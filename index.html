About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign under the supervision of Prof. Olgica Milenkovic. I received my M.S. in ECE from University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign in 2021. Previously, I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur with a major in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Machine Learning in 2018.

Research

My research interests lie broadly at the intersection of biology and mathematics. More specifically, I focus on applications of machine learning and big data to complex biological problems. My recent and ongoing projects include

  • Combinatorial promoter methylation pattern analysis in cancer.

  • Temporal and spatial charecterization of driver mutations in cancer.

  • Small-sample estimation of support and distribution of mutations in viral genomes.
